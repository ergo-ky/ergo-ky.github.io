About Me

Ky is a multi-displinary artist based out of Omaha, Nebraska. Mixing traditional crafts such as carpentry,gardening, and painting with experimental technologies such as augmented reality and blockchain consensus. She seeks to thread a continuous loop through these complimentart mediums.
Drawing motivation from local popup community events, Hackathon discord servers, and the ever-seemingly evolving space. Ky began to question the role she was to play society, and how her art could reflect this contemplation. Taking a step back and really focusing on the core of what she wanted to do. Ky found that her journey as an artist was just beginning.
Some projects that she has started along the way so far have been: illustrations, animations, and prints.
